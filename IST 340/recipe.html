Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup Refried Beans
  • 1 Large Brown Rice Tortilla
  • 1 cup Shredded Lettuce
  • ¼ cup Red Onion
  • ¼ cup Tomatoe
  • ¼ cup Salsa of your choice (I love Spicy Salsa the best!)
  • 2 tbsp Sour Cream or Shredded Cheese
  • Steak:
  • 1 serving Chopped Steak

Mexican Burrito

PREP TIME - 10 MINS     COOK TIME - 12 MINS     TOTAL TIME - 22 MINS

  1. First preheat oven to 350 then heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

  2. Spread Refried Beans onto Tortilla and pop into oven.

  3. Chop the Steak into small pieces into the bowl and add seasonings. Mix well before dumping in hot pan.

  4. Chop up Red onion, Tomatoe and Lettuce into bite-size pieces, while occasionally stiring Steak in pan.

  5. Add Veggies to pan and stir for 4-5 minutes, or until hot.

  6. Take out warmed Tortilla from oven, and top with Steak and Veggie mixture from the pan.

  7. Top with Salsa and Sour Cream.

  8. Don't forget to enjoy and keep eatin goood
